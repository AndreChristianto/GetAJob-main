OUR COMPANY

GetAJob was initially founded by a group of university students

for a group project in 2022 during the COVID pandemic.

GetAJob then developed into a fully developed web application

and became widely used by people all around indonesia.

GetAJob is a platform for finding thousands of services done

by hundreds of skilled freelancers. We aim to provide customers

a wide array of services for their needs while maintaining the quality

of the services. GetAJob is exclusive to Indonesian citizens.

This makes communication easier and more efficient, and also

increases the possibilities of gigs.

GET CONNECTED QUICKLY

GetAJob allows users to connect with each other, no matter where they are. For buyers,

all it takes is to explore GetAJob’s list of available sales to find what they’re looking for.

While Sellers, they only need to put up the things they want to sell in GetAJob and wait

until a customer arrives.
